Volatility often presents opportunity when properly reacted to however without appropriate adjustments volatility can be detrimental. Traditionally, increases in volatility creates shifts in sector momentum determined by the fundamental cause of the volatility shift. Identifying the cause however, is often an elusive and subjective matter.


Previous studies focused on the Chinese Equity Market have utilized sentiment analysis through news headlines to isolate potential drivers for increases in volatility. Utilizing this approach, causes could be identified and suggestions be made.


Developing upon this idea, our novel approach interpolates sentiment analysis with volatility values to identify periods where sentiment is driving markets and exclude periods where markets are behaving closer to a norm.
